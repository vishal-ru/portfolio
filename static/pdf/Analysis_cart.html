"Cart Abandonment Analysis: What Makes Shoppers Leave Without Buying?"

This project explores online shopping behavior to uncover why users abandon their carts before completing a purchase. Using device types, traffic sources, and revenue data, we identify key patterns and drop-off points. The analysis helps e-commerce businesses optimize user experience and improve conversion rates. Key insights are visualized through dashboards and data breakdowns for strategic decision-making.
